justin bieber

  • Justin Drew Bieber (/biːbər/ BEE-bər; born March 1, 1994)[1][2] is a Canadian singer.
  • He is recognized for his genre-melding musicianship and global influence in modern-day popular music.
  • Bieber was discovered by American record executive Scooter Braun and signed with RBMG Records in 2008,
  • gaining recognition with the release of his debut seven-track EP My World (2009) and soon establishing himself as a teen idol.
